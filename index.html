Welcome to Bacolod City

Welcome to Bacolod City, where every moment is an adventure waiting to be explored! Nestled in the heart of the Philippines, Bacolod beckons with its irresistible blend of vibrant culture, rich heritage, and exhilarating festivals. Dive into the heart of our city and immerse yourself in the pulsating energy of the world-famous MassKara Festival, where colorful parades and lively music set the stage for an unforgettable celebration of life. Indulge your senses in the culinary delights of the "Sugar Capital of the Philippines," where every dish tells a story of tradition and flavor. Explore our enchanting landscapes, from serene beaches to lush mountains, where adventure awaits at every turn. Discover our heritage sites and ancient churches, where history comes alive amidst stunning architecture and timeless beauty. Shop 'til you drop in our bustling markets and vibrant malls, and take home a piece of Bacolod to cherish forever. Come and experience the magic of Bacolod City today, where the spirit of fiesta and the warmth of Filipino hospitality await you!
